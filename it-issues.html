5 Common IT Issues Small Businesses Face (and How to Fix Them)

Small businesses often face IT challenges that disrupt their operations. Here are the top 5 issues and how to resolve them:

  1. Slow Networks: Caused by outdated hardware or poor configuration. Upgrade your router and ensure bandwidth is optimized.
  2. Email Problems: Misconfigured DNS or spam filtering. Ensure proper email server settings.
  3. Cybersecurity Threats: Use antivirus, firewalls, and employee training to avoid phishing attacks.
  4. Data Loss: Implement regular backups and cloud storage.
  5. Outdated Software: Schedule updates and automate patching.
