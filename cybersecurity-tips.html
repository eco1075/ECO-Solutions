Top Cybersecurity Tips to Protect Your Business in 2025

Cybersecurity is critical for small businesses. Follow these tips to stay safe:

  • Use strong, unique passwords with a password manager.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Regularly update and patch all systems.
  • Train staff to recognize phishing emails and scams.
  • Use endpoint security and firewall solutions.
